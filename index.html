Serious Computer Group (SCG) is the duo of Nina Bouchard and Evan Montpellier.

Founded in 2018 and based in Montreal, SCG works across a range of physical and digital disciplines, including interactive installation, photography and filmmaking, programming and electronics, sound and video art.

SCG's goal is to synthesize its diverse practices into novel hybrid aesthetic languages, balancing formal experimentation with an accessible sensibility and an appreciation for narrative.
